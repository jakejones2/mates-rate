How Do I Start?

Welcome to Mates Rate! This is an online game where you can rate your friends, family, and celebrities in funny categories.

Click 'Create Game' to host a new game that your friends can join.

To join another player's game, select 'Join Game'.

As the game host, you can skip rounds if people are taking too long! To do this, click the button 'Skip Round' once the game starts.
