How do I play?

Each round, one player is asked to think of a category that people or things can be judged in (e.g. speed, wisdom, cooking skills etc.).

Meanwhile, the remaining players must each think of a character (e.g. Mark Zuckerberg, Peter Pan, your friend).

In 'boring mode' the category is known in advance, but otherwise this is a mystery!

After all submissions are collected, players vote on a rating from 1 to 100 for each character in the chosen category. Discussion and debate are encouraged.

At the end of each round, characters receive an average score based on player votes. The highest score wins, and players are awarded points based on the performance of their character.

Make sure cookies are enabled, and enjoy!
